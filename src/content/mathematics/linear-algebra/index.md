## Introduction to Linear Algebra

Linear Algebra is central to almost all areas of modern mathematics and heavily applied in computer science, especially in domains dealing with large amounts of data, multidimensional spaces, and transformations.

### Core Topics

1.  **Vectors and Spaces**: Understanding how to represent and manipulate points and directions in multi-dimensional space.
2.  **Matrices and Linear Transformations**: Matrices are the standard way to represent transformations (like rotations and scaling in computer graphics) and systems of linear equations.
3.  **Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors**: Essential for understanding dynamic systems, dimensionality reduction (like PCA in machine learning), and the PageRank algorithm.
