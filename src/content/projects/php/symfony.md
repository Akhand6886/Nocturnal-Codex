### Why contribute to Symfony?
Symfony is the foundation of many other PHP projects (including Laravel and Drupal). It is highly decoupled and follows strict object-oriented principles. It is the best place to learn deep architectural patterns and the internal workings of HTTP, routing, and dependency injection.

### Contribution Tips
- **Decoupling**: Focus on making components as independent as possible.
- **Documentation**: Symfony's documentation is top-tier. Even fixing a typo in the docs is a valuable contribution.
- **Strictness**: Be prepared for a very rigorous code review process.
- **BC Break**: Avoid breaking changes at all costs; Symfony values stability.
