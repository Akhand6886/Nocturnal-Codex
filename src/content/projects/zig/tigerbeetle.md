## Why Contribute?
TigerBeetle is one of the most ambitious projects written in Zig. It pushes the boundaries of database performance and safety using Zig's unique features like `comptime`.

## Contribution Tips
- Read the project's "Design Docs" first; they are extremely detailed.
- Zig experience is highly recommended before diving into the core consensus logic.
- Look for `good first issue` labels.
