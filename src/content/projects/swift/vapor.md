### Why contribute to Vapor?
Vapor brings Swift to the server. It's built on top of SwiftNIO (Apple's high-performance networking engine). Contributing here teaches you about non-blocking I/O, database drivers (Fluent), and server-side Swift architecture.

### Contribution Tips
- **Fluent**: Contribute to the ORM by adding features or fixing bugs in specific database drivers (PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQLite).
- **Leaf**: Help improve the templating engine.
- **Middleware**: Write reusable middleware for things like authentication or logging.
