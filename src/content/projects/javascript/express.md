### Why contribute to Express?
Express is the backbone of the Node.js web ecosystem. It's relatively small and focused, making it a great place to learn about middleware patterns and HTTP handling in JS.

### Contribution Tips
- **Middleware**: Study how middleware works in the core.
- **Documentation**: Express is currently undergoing a documentation overhaul; lots of opportunities there!
- **Tests**: Helping to migrate tests to modern frameworks is a common need.
