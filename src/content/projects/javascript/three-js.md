### Why contribute to Three.js?
If you love graphics and visual effects, Three.js is the place to be. You'll learn about 3D math, shaders, and high-performance rendering in the browser.

### Contribution Tips
- **Examples**: Three.js has hundreds of examples. Fixing or adding a new one is the best way to get started.
- **Math Utilities**: Small fixes to vectors or matrices are common.
- **Documentation**: Clarifying the API for complex components like Shaders.
