### Why contribute to Pure?
Pure is the gold standard for minimal Zsh prompts. It's written with an extreme focus on performance and "Asynchronous" execution (so it doesn't slow down your terminal). Contributing here teaches you about Zsh async workers and subshells.

### Contribution Tips
- **Performance**: Help ensure the prompt stays fast even on massive Git repos.
- **Features**: Add a small, non-intrusive feature like background job indicators.
- **Cross-Shell**: Help improve the compatibility with different Zsh versions or frameworks.
- **Code Quality**: Pure has a very high bar for code quality and simplicity; studying its source is a great lesson in minimalism.
