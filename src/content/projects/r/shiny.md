### Why contribute to Shiny?
Shiny is the foundation of interactive data science in the R world. It features a unique reactive programming model. Contributing here teaches you about reactive graphs, web sockets, and how to bridge R and JavaScript.

### Contribution Tips
- **Reactivity**: Master the concepts of `obsere`, `reactive`, and `isolate`.
- **JS/R Bridge**: If you know JavaScript, help improve the custom input/output bindings.
- **UI Components**: Contribute new UI elements or layouts to the `bslib` or `thematic` packages.
- **Performance**: Help optimize the reactive execution engine for large-scale apps.
