### Why contribute to the Tidyverse?
The Tidyverse is the gold standard for data science in R. Contributing to packages like `ggplot2`, `dplyr`, or `tidyr` allows you to impact millions of researchers and analysts. You'll learn about functional programming in R and the "Tidy Data" philosophy.

### Contribution Tips
- **Issue Labels**: Look for the `good first issue` or `help wanted` labels across the various sub-repos.
- **Style Guide**: Ensure your code follows the `tidyverse` style guide strictly.
- **Roxygen2**: Master the use of Roxygen2 for in-code documentation.
- **Examples**: Improving the examples in the help files is one of the most useful contributions you can make.
