## Why Contribute?
Phoenix is the gold standard for real-time web applications. Contributing here means working on high-performance networking, WebSockets (Channels), and the legendary LiveView.

## Contribution Tips
- Familiarize yourself with the BEAM VM and Elixir processes.
- Start with documentation or small bug fixes in `phoenix_live_view`.
- Check the issues labeled `help wanted`.
