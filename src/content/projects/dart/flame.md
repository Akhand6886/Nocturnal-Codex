### Why contribute to Flame?
Flame is the leading game engine for the Flutter ecosystem. It's fun, visual, and has a very welcoming community. It's a great place to learn about the "Game Loop" pattern, sprite rendering, and physics in Dart.

### Contribution Tips
- **Bridge Packages**: Flame has bridges for Forge2D (physics) and Tiled (maps). Help improve these integrations.
- **Tutorials**: Write a tutorial on how to build a simple mechanic (like jumping or health bars).
- **Core Improvements**: Help optimize the rendering pipeline or the component system.
