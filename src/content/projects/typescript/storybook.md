### Why contribute to Storybook?
Storybook sits at the intersection of all major frontend frameworks (React, Vue, Angular). It's a great project to see how to build tools that support a diverse ecosystem.

### Contribution Tips
- **Documentation**: They have a massive "docs-athon" culture.
- **Add-ons**: Explore the add-on ecosystem; many small bugs exist there.
- **Framework Support**: Help test Storybook against newer versions of frontend libraries.
