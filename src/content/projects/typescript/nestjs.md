### Why contribute to NestJS?
NestJS is the go-to for enterprise-grade TypeScript backends. It heavily uses decorators and dependency injection. It's a great project to study advanced TS patterns and SOLID principles.

### Contribution Tips
- **CLI**: Many good first issues are in the Nest CLI.
- **Documentation**: Their docs are world-class; help keep them that way.
- **Modules**: Help with community-driven modules (e.g., GraphQL, Microservices).
