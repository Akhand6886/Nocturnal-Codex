### Why contribute to VS Code?
Contributing to the world's most popular editor is a badge of honor. You'll work on one of the largest and most complex TypeScript codebases in existence, learning about Electron, performance optimization, and extension APIs.

### Contribution Tips
- **Extensions**: Often, the best way to "contribute" is by fixing issues in core extensions (like the built-in HTML or CSS extensions).
- **Icons & UI**: Small UI tweaks or icon fixes are good starting points.
- **Good First Issues**: They are very diligent about tagging these; search for them specifically!
