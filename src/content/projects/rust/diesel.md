### Why contribute to Diesel?
Diesel is the most robust ORM in the Rust ecosystem. It uses Rust's type system to ensure that your database queries are correct before they even run. Contributing here gives you a deep dive into Rust's traits, generics, and macros.

### Contribution Tips
- **Backends**: Help improve support for specific database backends (PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQLite).
- **Migration Tooling**: Improve the `diesel_cli` tool for managing migrations.
- **Documentation**: Diesel has a steep learning curve; improving the guides is a great service to the community.
