### Why contribute to ASP.NET Core?
ASP.NET Core is the backbone of the modern Microsoft web stack. It is one of the fastest web frameworks in the world. Contributing here teaches you about high-performance middleware, dependency injection, and modern C# features like `Span<T>` and `Memory<T>`.

### Contribution Tips
- **Performance**: Many issues are focused on reducing allocations or improving throughput.
- **Components**: You can contribute to specific areas like SignalR (real-time), Blazor (web UI), or Kestrel (the web server).
- **Documentation**: The `dotnet/docs` repo is also a great place to start.
- **Benchmarks**: Help maintain the framework's position on the TechEmpower benchmarks.
