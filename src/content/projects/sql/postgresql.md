### Why contribute to PostgreSQL?
PostgreSQL is the standard by which all other databases are measured. It is a massive C project with a highly rigorous development process. Contributing here teaches you about query optimization, storage engines, and ACID compliance at a world-class level.

### Contribution Tips
- **Mailing List**: Like Git, Postgres uses a mailing list for development. Read the "Developer FAQ" on their wiki first.
- **SQL Standards**: Ensure your knowledge of the SQL:2023 standard is sharp.
- **Extensions**: Developing a new extension (like PostGIS or pgvector) is often easier than contributing to the core engine.
- **Benchmarks**: Help analyze performance regressions between versions.
