---
title: "Gradient, Jacobian, Hessian"
description: "Learn about the derivatives of functions of multiple variables, which are central to optimizing machine learning models."
---
These are multi-variable generalizations of the derivative. The Gradient points in the direction of the steepest ascent of a function, the Jacobian is the matrix of all first-order partial derivatives of a vector-valued function, and the Hessian is a square matrix of second-order partial derivatives.
