Package managers automate the process of installing, upgrading, configuring, and removing third-party packages and dependencies. They are a critical part of the modern development workflow, saving you from having to manually manage the libraries your project depends on. `npm` is the default package manager for Node.js, with `yarn` and `pnpm` being popular alternatives.
