## 1. The Plan Stage

The **Plan** stage is the foundation of the DevOps lifecycle. This is where teams define business value and requirements. It involves creating a backlog of tasks, prioritizing them, and planning sprints.

### Key Activities:
- **Requirement Gathering:** Understanding what the customer wants.
- **Backlog Management:** Using tools like Jira or Trello to track user stories and bugs.
- **Agile Methodologies:** Adopting frameworks like Scrum or Kanban to organize work in an iterative fashion.

Effective planning ensures that the development team is working on features that deliver the most value to the users and the business.
